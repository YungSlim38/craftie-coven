About me

Hi, I'm so happy you're here!!!

I’m Emily, a quiet soul living in London, happiest with a movie on & craft supplies in hand. I've always found comfort in making things by hand, turning simple materials into something beautiful, thoughtful, and made with love.

For me, crafting isn't just a hobby, it's a little pocket of calm in a busy world. When life sometimes gets hard I love slowing down, getting lost in the creative process, and adding tiny details that make each piece feel special, hoping I can make someone else’s day just a little more better.

This little corner of the internet is where I get to share the things that bring me joy. I hope that when one of my creations finds its way to you, it brings a little warmth, a smile, or a touch of magic to you everyday.

Thank you for stopping by and for supporting Craftie Coven, it truly means the world to me.
